ABOUT US

A Brief Introduction


Welcome to Jersey Stories, the premier online sportswear store in Bangladesh. We have been in business since 2022, when we started out as a small Facebook page called "Jersey Stories". Since then, we have grown significantly, and today we are proud to offer a wide range of high-quality football jerseys from official European football clubs and international teams.

Our jerseys are manufactured in Thailand, and are made with the finest materials to ensure that they are of the highest quality. In addition to selling standard jerseys, we also offer customization services for local sports teams in Bangladesh. With our unique designs and top-quality fabric, we can create the perfect jersey for your team. At Jersey Stories, we are more than just a sportswear store.
Thank you for choosing Jersey Stories as your go-to online sportswear store. We appreciate your time and look forward to serving you in the future.

Our Policy


At Jersey Stories, we are dedicated to ensuring 100% customer satisfaction. We appreciate your business and want to express our gratitude to you. If for any reason you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, please let us know, and we will do our best to make it right. To initiate a return, please follow the guidelines outlined below.

Conditions for Return:


  • The return request must be submitted within 3 business days from the date of item delivery.
  • All original tags (including hand tags, brand tags, and other tags that come with the product), user manual, warranty cards, certificates of authenticity, and accessories should be included.
  • The product must be unused, unworn, and have all its original tags attached.
  • It should be free from any flaws, damages, or signs of wear.
  • Fashion products can be tried on to check the fit, but they should still be in an unworn condition.
  • Returns must be received within 14 days from the date of receipt.

Conditions for Refund:


  • Upon receiving and inspecting the returned item, we will assess its condition to ensure it meets the return policy requirements.
  • If the returned product meets all the conditions, you can choose to receive a refund or exchange the item for another product of equal value.
  • Refunds will be processed using the original payment method.
  • In the case of an exchange, the new product will be shipped to you once the returned item is received and processed.
